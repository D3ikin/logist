About the LogistPRO

The LogistPRO TMS was developed by S2B Group to simplify and automate the work of shippers.

LogistPRO combines all the necessary tools for shipping logistics control, so you don’t have to use additional services.

Our approach greatly simplifies the tasks of searching reliable carriers and communication with them, organization the process of loading and unloading goods, tracking them in transit, obtaining all analytical data on cargo shipping, and more.
